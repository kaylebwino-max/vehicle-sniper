FAQ
Questions people ask
How is this different from just searching Craigslist?

We do the searching and filtering for you. Craigslist shows you everything — including overpriced listings, salvage titles, and outright scams. We show you only what's worth your time.

What if a car is already sold by the time I see it?

We update daily and try to catch removed listings. If a car is gone, it comes out of the next email. The best deals go fast — that's why we prioritize speed.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel any time from the Stripe customer portal. No questions asked, no lock-in.

Do you cover my area?

We search nationwide by default. You can set your preferred radius and city when you subscribe. Rural areas may have more limited results.

Is this legal?

Yes. We use public data sources and follow Craigslist's terms of service. We're not hacking anything — we're just faster at reading public listings than a human.
